The Lost Key

One afternoon, a young boy named Sam was playing in his backyard when he found an old key buried in the dirt. Curious, he wondered what it could unlock.

He searched the entire house, trying to find where the key fit. Just as he was about to give up, he noticed a small, hidden door behind the bookshelf in the attic. With trembling hands, Sam inserted the key and turned it slowly. The door creaked open, revealing a room filled with forgotten treasures.

His adventure had only just begun.
